City Latitude vs. Humidity

Description and Analysis:

There doesn't appear to be a clear relationship between latitude and humidity. Locations reported in the Northern Hemisphere did record lower humidity levels than the Southern Hemisphere, which perhaps is not that surprising given the location of tropical rainforests and siilar landscapes below 0 degrees latitude.
